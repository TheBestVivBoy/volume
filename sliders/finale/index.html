Episode 67

All for now

A long, long time ago, a creator named vivancodes set out to revive one of the internet's most pointless competitions: creating the worst possible way to change your volume.

Encouraged by a friend to try and make one, the first slider appeared and unexpectedly took off.

One ridiculous video quickly led to another, and suddenly a series formed around a single line on a page. Now, I have over 70 million total views, 35k+ followers and almost 5 million likes.

Over time, the sliders drifted far beyond the regular line and into games, locks, batteries, pumps, dogs, chess boards, Minecraft mods, and into all sorts of strange corners of the internet.

From the 2017 Reddit contest to this 67th episode, each video was a small joke designed to make you, the audience, have a laugh

I couldn't have done it without you guys. All these ideas and inspiration for future projects were fuelled by your comments on my videos.

This doesn't mean I am going to stop making videos. I'm just going to stop uploading daily and switch to a “when inspiration strikes" style of creating.

The sliders will still be accessible on my website, https://vivancodes.com/, it's all free but if you have enjoyed the series then please consider leaving a donation to support it and future content!

Thank you for being part of this journey.
